{{< note title="Maxwell Equation (Integral)">}}
* **Gauss' Law**:

$$ \qquad \iint_{\partial \Omega} \mathbf{E} \cdot d\mathbf{S} = 4 \pi \iiint_{\Omega} \rho dV $$ 

* **Gauss' Law for Magnetism**
$$ \qquad \iint_{\partial \Omega} \mathbf{B} \cdot d\mathbf{S} = 0 $$
